Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Supply Chain Disaster ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use Supply Chain Disaster (the "Game" or "Service") at supplychaindisaster.com. By using the Service you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email address — provided when you save your progress or make a purchase. Used to send your progress checkpoint, purchase confirmation, and to restore access on a new device.
  • Order ID — generated by our payment processor (Lemon Squeezy) when you purchase Standard Edition or the Expansion Bundle. Used solely to verify and restore your premium access.
  • Contact form submissions — name, email, and message you send through our contact form. Used to respond to your enquiry.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage analytics — via Google Analytics 4 (GA4). We collect anonymised event data such as page views, game session events (game_started, round_completed, cta_clicked), and device/browser type. IP addresses are anonymised. You can opt out via your browser's Do Not Track setting or a GA4 opt-out extension.
  • Local storage — game progress (turns, chapter, score) and premium access status are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the "Save Progress" or "Restore Access" features.

1.3 Payment Information

We do not collect or store payment card details. All transactions are handled by Lemon Squeezy (web) or RevenueCat (Android). Their privacy policies govern the handling of your payment data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and restore your premium access across devices.
  • To send transactional emails (purchase confirmation, progress checkpoints).
  • To respond to contact form enquiries.
  • To measure and improve game performance and conversion through anonymised analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

  • Email addresses collected for progress saves or purchases are retained for as long as your account is active or as required to fulfil our legal obligations.
  • Analytics data is retained per Google's standard GA4 retention settings (default: 14 months).
  • LocalStorage data remains on your device until you clear your browser data or explicitly delete it.

5. Cookies

The Game uses Google Analytics cookies (_ga, _ga_*) to distinguish users and sessions for analytics. These are first-party cookies set on the supplychaindisaster.com domain. No third-party advertising or tracking cookies are set. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though this may affect analytics functionality.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have rights including:

  • Access — request a copy of personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data.
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@nexttracksystems.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed by our service providers in the United States or other countries. Where required, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (such as standard contractual clauses) for international transfers of personal data.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of data transmitted to us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be indicated by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
